How it works

Keep the workflow simple: choose a location, pull weather, produce predictions, and output an actionable prep plan.

1
Set location
Pick your cafe location to retrieve local forecasts and historical weather patterns.
2
Model prediction
Use weather signals + historical sales (future integration) to estimate demand and waste risk.
3
Prep plan
Get daily recommended prep quantities and notes (e.g., “rain → hot items”).

Designed for small teams

No complex configuration. Clear KPIs, explainable drivers, and practical recommendations your team can follow.
